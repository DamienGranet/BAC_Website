The band

Who we are

Bronze Age Collapse are a four-piece from Canberra making grunge-inspired hard rock. Imagine if some kids spent all their time listening to Led Zeppelin, Nirvana, Soundgarden, Alice in Chains, Incubus, Pink Floyd, Black Sabbath and Karnivool instead of practicing.

On 1 January 2026 we released our debut album, Game of Charades — nine tracks spanning everything from Voidsteer's raw energy to Bittersweet's soaring guitar solos. Our song The World was picked by Woroni Radio among the best local gold of 2026 so far — read the article.

If you don't know what the Bronze Age Collapse was, don't look it up; it's better for us if the word association takes you straight to our music.
