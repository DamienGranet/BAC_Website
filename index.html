The band

Who we are

Bronze Age Collapse are a four-piece from Canberra making grunge-inspired hard rock — the kind of thing that happens when you raise a band on Led Zeppelin, Nirvana, Soundgarden, Alice in Chains, Incubus, Pink Floyd, Black Sabbath and Karnivool and then hand them amplifiers.

On 1 January 2026 we released our debut album, Game of Charades — nine tracks spanning everything from Voidsteer to Phil's Big Night Out. Our song The World was picked by Woroni Radio among the best local gold of 2026 so far — read the article.

Yes, we're named after the collapse of Mediterranean civilisation circa 1200 BC. No, we don't take ourselves that seriously.
